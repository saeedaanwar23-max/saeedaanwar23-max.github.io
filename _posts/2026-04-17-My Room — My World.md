My Room — My World
How Space Affects You
"Creating your own space" — I did not understand this phrase at first. But when I set up a small corner of my room just for studying, I understood. That corner had a table, a lamp, and a few essentials. But it sent me a signal every time I sat down: this is where studying happens. Space has its own psychology.
What Should Be on Your Desk
I learned that a clean desk leads to focused studying. Only the essentials — one notebook, one pen, a water bottle, and whatever you are currently studying. Phone away, extra papers away. It seems like a small thing but the difference is enormous. When the desk is clean, the mind feels a little cleaner too.
You Can Create a Study Space Anywhere
You do not need a large room or expensive furniture. A chair, a flat surface, and some time — that is all. If possible, sit near a window — natural light does not tire your eyes and keeps your mood fresh. Creating your own space is itself a commitment — come to this space and do the work. 🌟
