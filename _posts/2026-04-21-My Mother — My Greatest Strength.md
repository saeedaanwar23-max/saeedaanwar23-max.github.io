My Mother — My Greatest Strength
What Was Never Said Out Loud
My mother never said "I love you so much" — but she said it countless times in other ways. Waking up early to make breakfast, praying before every exam, the quiet worry in her eyes — all of that was her love, expressed not in words but in actions. I understood this late, but when I did, my heart filled completely.
Her Sacrifices
My mother gave up a great deal for me. Time, rest, her own desires — all for me. I never saw all of that directly, but when I think about it now, I understand. Every mother's life holds a silent sacrifice that children only grasp when they grow a little older. With that understanding comes a feeling — now it is my turn to do something.
Making Her Happy Is My Goal
Whenever I feel like giving up on my studies, one thing comes to mind: my mother's eyes when I told her I would do well. I made a promise — not to her, but to myself. Her happiness is my reward. And that reward is bigger than any exam, more meaningful than any mark sheet. 💖
