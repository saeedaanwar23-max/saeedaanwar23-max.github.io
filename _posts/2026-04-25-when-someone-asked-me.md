When Someone Asked Me — What Will You Do With Education?
That Question
I have heard this question many times in my life — sometimes directly, sometimes wrapped in polite words. "What will you do with all this studying?" This question is never just a question. Behind it sits an assumption — that a girl's education has no purpose or has a limit. The first time I heard it, I went quiet. But something rose inside me — an answer, a decision.
My Answer
I decided then that I would answer this question with my life, not my words. I would study, learn, earn, and inspire others. This answer was not for anyone else — it was for myself. Education showed me that the answer to my questions lives only inside me — and that I am also the answer to questions that come from outside.
To Every Girl Being Asked This
If you have been asked this question — you will keep being asked it. But every time you walk into class, every time you write notes, every time you sit an exam — that is your answer. Your presence is the answer. Education is your right, not anyone's favour. And one day the world will see what answer you gave to this question. 🌸💪
