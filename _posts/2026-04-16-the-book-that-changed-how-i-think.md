The Book That Changed How I Think
When I Fell in Love With Reading
I remember the day I read a book for the very first time by choice — not because it was on the syllabus, but because I genuinely wanted to know. The book was simple, but what was inside it stayed with me all night. That moment made me realize that books are not just pages — they are doorways into different lives and different worlds.
That One Book
One book showed me that what happens in the world is not just luck — it is the result of thinking, effort, and taking the right step at the right time. It made me feel, for the first time, that I have control over my own life. Any book that challenges your thinking, that asks you difficult questions — that is a book worth reading.
Why Every Student Needs a Non-Academic Book
If you only read textbooks, you are only learning the syllabus. But when you read stories, biographies, and books of ideas, you are learning life itself. I tell every student: keep one non-academic book nearby at all times. It will give you something no lesson plan ever can — a new perspective. 📚
